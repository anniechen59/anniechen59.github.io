The Challenge: Beyond the "Black-Box"

Most AI coaching tools suffer from "Black-Box Evaluation" and "Keyword Rigidity". If a speaker uses a synonym instead of the exact word on a slide, traditional systems fail. PrepMaster AI was engineered to deliver quantitative, traceable, and objective feedback through multi-modal analysis.

Key Innovations:

  • Transparent Evaluation: 3:4:3 weighted scoring model (Content, Fluency, Tone).
  • Intelligent Alignment: Hierarchical validation ensures meanings are tracked, not just exact words.
  • Actionable Metrics: Insights into WPM, filler-word density, and pitch variability.

Technical Architecture & Three-Layer Validation

At the core is a sophisticated engine designed to ensure highly accurate content tracking, even with nuanced speech:

  1. Level 1: Exact & Lemma Match — Utilizes nltk.WordNetLemmatizer to normalize spoken words (e.g., "analyzing" to "analyze").
  2. Level 2: Phrase & Substring Match — Detects multi-word phrases and handles punctuation/white space cleansing.
  3. Level 3: Semantic Vector Match — Employs sentence-transformers (all-MiniLM-L6-v2) with a 0.15 Weak Threshold to identify synonyms and paraphrasing, significantly reducing false negatives.

  • Auto-Sync: Intelligent alignment of speech to specific slides.
  • Metric Report: Detailed slide-by-slide scores and coaching advice.
